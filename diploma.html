Study Diploma in Engineering at United


Diploma in Engineering in United is provided in these fields:

Diploma in Civil Engineering:The course provides education and training in the areas planning, designing, construction, field work and maintenance of physical and natural environment.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering:The course provides education and training in the areas heat utilization or machine design. Machine design is more focused on hardware, including automobile engines, computer and washing machine.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering:The course provides education and training in Electrical Engineering is a field of engineering that generally deals with the concepts and machines used in day to day life of a common person like computer, electrical power supply and machines etc


Eligibility


(Eligibility for admission in Three Year Diploma)Program (Polytechnic Courses) as per B.T.E., Lucknow norms. The Candidates should have passed 10th standard examination from a recognized Board of Education with a minimum of 35% percent of marks.
